# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and other developers evaluating my QA engineering skills and portfolio. They are looking for clear evidence of technical competence, problem-solving, and attention to detail.

## Product Purpose
To showcase my professional experience, skills, and projects as a Senior QA Engineer. It serves as an interactive resume that demonstrates both my technical capabilities and my aesthetic sensibilities.

## Brand Personality
High-craft, cinematic, dark-mode-first, kinetic, minimal. Professional yet technologically sophisticated.

## Anti-references
Generic portfolio templates, light mode defaults, overly complex navigation, "AI slop" or cliche SaaS designs (e.g. hero-metric templates, identical card grids).

## Design Principles
- Practice what you preach (high quality, bug-free).
- Show, don't tell.
- Kinetic minimalism (motion adds value, not noise).
- Expert confidence.
- Accessibility is non-negotiable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliant, readable contrast in dark mode, clear focus states, and considerations for reduced motion.
